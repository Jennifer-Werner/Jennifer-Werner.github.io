STEP ONE

Make sure that you are eligible for the position. You should be able to commit to the project and work in a team, dedicating your time to creating lessons and study materials or fulfilling your other responsibilities.

STEP TWO

Fill in your application in the link provided on the page. This will be the first step of your selection process. Make sure to provide elaborated and detailed answers to help us to provide the most eligible position for your experience.

STEP THREE

Go through an interview with one of your members that would let us get to know you and introduce you to your responsibilities as part of the team, answering your questions and providing guidance and instructions in the beginning of your path.

STEP FOUR

Start teaching or help the team with creating study materials or recruiting new teachers. One of our curators will tell you about the educational system of the project and help you with connecting with the students and planning out the lessons.
